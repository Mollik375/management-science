Knowledge Gained

Throughout this course, I learned how Management Science uses mathematical and analytical methods to support decision-making. I studied Decision Trees, the Pinocchio Problem, the Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP), Picking Heuristics, and Linear Programming. These concepts demonstrated how complex business problems can be solved systematically and efficiently.

Skills Developed

This course helped me improve my problem-solving, critical thinking, and quantitative analysis skills. I learned how to evaluate alternatives, optimize resources, analyze networks, and apply mathematical models to real-world situations. I also gained experience presenting technical concepts through web-based projects.

Personal Reflection

The most challenging topics were the Traveling Salesman Problem and Linear Programming because they required careful calculations and logical reasoning. However, working through examples and project activities helped me better understand these concepts and their practical applications.

I was surprised by how Management Science techniques can be applied to everyday business decisions such as transportation planning, logistics management, inventory control, and resource allocation. These tools provide valuable insights that can improve efficiency and support better decision-making.

Conclusion

Overall, this course strengthened my analytical thinking and introduced me to powerful decision-making techniques. The knowledge and skills gained from Management Science will be valuable in both my future studies and professional career.
